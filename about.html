About Us

Bengal School of Hotel Management was established in April 2019 in Durgapur, West Bengal. Regular sessions started in September of the following year. A society registered under the Society Registration Act governs the Institute (1961). Registration No. of the Society under Society's Registration Act. 1961 â€“ S0008130 of 2019/2020. The Institute and its programs were created to prepare promising and talented young boys and girls for careers in the hospitality industry. Food Production, Patisserie, and Food & Beverage Service are just a few specific fields covering the hospitality and hotel operations spectrum. The Institute seeks to adequately equip students to learn positions of responsibility in many aspects of the hospitality industry under the skilled leadership of a trained and experienced teaching team. In other words, to provide them with a professional career. We will uncover and interact with new ideas that will lead to a bright and prosperous future by working together.


Mission: To convey lessons and training in modern and scientific techniques of management of hotels and catering industry. To provide the knowledge of the nutritional value of food items and their development work. Offering consultancy in the field of hospitality & tourism will help students to overcome the situation.

Vision: By providing instructions and training in all the crafts and skills, all the branches of knowledge both theoretical and applied, and all the organizational and management techniques which are required for the efficient functioning of the hotel and catering establishment of all kinds, as well as institutional feeding programs in the industrial establishment and similar organization.
