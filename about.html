About Us

Bengal School of Hotel Management (BSHM), Durgapur, is a new venture in West Bengal that aspires to train young, motivated boys and girls who want to develop management, hard, and soft skills to excel in the country's hospitality business. BSHM, Durgapur promises the greatest education, together with “State-of-the-Art” amenities and a contemporary, professional attitude.


Vision: West Bengal has such potential, we envision establishing a hotel management school to entice aspiring hoteliers from Eastern India and other parts of the country to visit Bengal and explore its potential.

Mission: Catering businesses are multiplying rapidly as a result of urbanisation and industrialisation. A unique task of great significance is the creation of adequate services for feeding the numerous and increasing amount of employees engaged in industrial production.
